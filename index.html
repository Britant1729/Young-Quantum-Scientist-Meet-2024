Welcome to the International Young Quantum Scientists’ Meet 2024

The event will provide a platform where enthusiastic young researchers working in the domain of Quantum Communication, Quantum Computing, Quantum Sensing, Quantum Materials, and Quantum Optics can share their results and explore avenues for new collaborations.

Date: September 16 – 20, 2024

Timing: IST 6 pm to 9 pm (online mode only)

Confirmed Young Speakers

  • Dr. Abhishek Shukla, University of Hasselt, Belgium
  • Dr. Kishore Thapliyal, Palacký University, Olomouc, Czech Republic
  • Dr. Chitra Shukla, Université du Luxembourg
  • Mr. Vaisakh Mannalath, University of Vigo, Spain
  • Dr. Priya Malpani, CDOT, India
