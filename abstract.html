List of Accepted Abstracts

1. Optical and electrical investigation of chemical doping in monolayer MoS2 for optoelectronic device applications

Name: Deyashini Mukherjee

Affiliation: Indian Institute of Technology Bombay

Abstract: This study investigates the impact of chemical doping on the optical and electrical properties of monolayer MoS2, which is crucial for developing efficient optoelectronic devices. Through advanced techniques, the research explores how doping modifies the bandgap and carrier concentration, enhancing the material's application in photodetectors and transistors.

3. Quenching electronic conduction in Cu-doped Bi2Te3 thin films through a metal to insulator transition

Name: Vinayak Soni

Affiliation: Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research
